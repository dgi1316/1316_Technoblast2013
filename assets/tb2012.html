TechnoBlast-2012, a project competition in Engineering/Technology and Management for colleges located in Delhi-NCR was held on November 24th, 2013. We received large number of projects from various colleges. Out of 120 projects received, 72 projects were shortlisted by a group of experts in specific domains. The evaluation was done in the areas of innovativeness, societal value, commercial viability and futuristic scope. This project competition was basically towards innovativeness and a small step to make students realize the importance of innovation.
