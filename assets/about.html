Dronacharya Group of Institutions

Dronacharya Group of Institutions affiliated to Mahamaya Technical University (MTU), Noida, is one of the prime engineering institutions of India. The institute is located at Knowledge Park-3, Greater Noida amidst picturesque surroundings. It has a widespread, aesthetic and sprawling campus mimicking a Gurukul of great learning. The infrastructure has been designed to cater to the requirements of modern day teaching wherein students can develop their creative and innovative skills. The institute provides degree in B.Tech Engineering and two post graduate degrees in MBA and MCA. The engineering degree comprise of the following:

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Computer and Information Technology
  • Electronics and Computer Engineering

Entrepreneurship and Creativity are the focus areas for the development of students. Research activities are factored in the academic curriculum. Student driven projects have been uploaded on various National and International level platforms. Students have received recognition and awards on more than 30 platforms at various levels in the last one year. Conduct of workshops, seminars, symposiums, guest lectures etc. are regular features of the institute.

The institute publishes International Journal of Engineering, Sciences and Management consisting of latest research papers authored by distinguished persons of international stature. Huge amount of intellectual capital exists in terms of authoring books, publication of research papers in national and international journals, attending of conferences and seminars and pursuing of doctoral degrees by faculty members.

The institute boasts of excellent placement record of the students of all branches. The percentage placement in the last 2 years have been 91 and 74 respectively. Student chapter of IEEE, ISTE, SPEED-IUCEE, NEN etc. exists wherein large number of events and activities are organized. There is strong partnership with Industry and FICCI, CII, DST, INAE, CDC etc.

The Vision and Mission of the institute are:

Vision

To impart Quality Education, to give an enviable growth to seekers of learning, to groom them as World Class Engineers and Managers competent to match the expanding expectations of the Corporate World has been our ever enlarging vision extending to new horizons since the inception of Dronacharya College of Engineering.

Mission

We, at Dronacharya College of Engineering, are absolutely committed to serve the society and improve the mode of life by imparting high quality education in the field of Engineering and Management catering to the explicit and implicit needs of the students, society, humanity and industry. ‘Shiksha evam Sahayata’ i.e. Education and Help are the two words etched on our banner soaring higher year after year. Our goal is to continuously improve the healthy learning environment and facilities being provided.
