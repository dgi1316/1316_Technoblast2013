Technoblast 2013

National Level Project Competition

It is a well known fact that creativity and innovation is based in deep rooted convictions and not merely on feeble inconsistent intuitions. Entrepreneurship and creativity are undoubtedly the holy grail of 21st century – hotly pursued and extremely elusive. This is the reason why countries like China, Korea, Japan and India have included creativity and research as a major goal of educational reform efforts. To meet the challenge of technology diffusion and interdisciplinary innovations, the college has given foremost importance to projects to be carried out by students. The emergence of this important aspect has been organizing National level project competition – TECHNOBLAST. The inaugural event TECHNOBLAST-2012 was held on 24 Nov 2012 wherein more than 120 projects from different domains participated in the competition. Prizes worth Rs. 1 lac were given to the winning projects as decided by a panel of experts from industry and academia. 1st prize of Rs. 51,000, 2nd prize of Rs. 25,000, 3rd prize of Rs. 10,000 and seven consolation prizes of Rs. 2,000 each were distributed. Dr. Sanjay Bajpai, Director, Dept. of Science & Technology, Govt. of India was the Chief Guest of the function. The competition was a great success opening the gates for the event to be organized every year.

TECHNOBLAST-2013 is scheduled on 23 Nov 2013. This is a National level project competition and more than 500 projects from colleges across the country are expected to participate. The prize money has been made more attractive and the competition is expected to be a cliff hanger. A highly qualified and worthy members of the jury panel will be evaluating the projects on its merit in terms of innovativeness, societal values, commercial viability and interdisciplinary mix. Exceptional and meaningful projects may be considered for incubation by Venture Capitalists. Companies of repute have been invited to display their products in the competition for exposition of students to the latest trends in technology.
